About

I am a researcher specialising in digital sovereignty and the extraterritorial enforcement of EU digital laws, with a particular interest in how regulatory power, legal design, and international cooperation shape the EU’s ability to project its norms beyond its borders. My work examines the evolving architecture of global digital governance, the limits of cross‑border enforcement, and the strategies through which the EU seeks to operationalise its regulatory influence in an increasingly fragmented digital sphere and amid broader issues linked to changes in geopolitics.

I am currently affiliated with the University of St Andrews (Scotland).
